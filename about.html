RVS College of Arts and Science


Rathinavel Subramaniam College of Arts and Science is started as first self-financing college under Bharathiar University in 1986. Within a brief span of two decades the college has developed as one of the largest self-financing colleges in Coimbatore region in terms of student strength, number of academic programmes and faculty strength. The college is managed by RVS Trust. Rathnavel Subramaniam College of Arts and Science, affiliated to Bharathiar University, Coimbatore, offer career-oriented education leading to graduation, post-graduation and research degrees in Arts and Science disciplines. Founded on 31st October, 1986, it has grown exuberantly to become one of the biggest self- financing colleges. The college was accorded autonomous status in 2004 by UGC and re-accredited in 2014 with an 'A' Grade further reaccredited in 2023 with A+ Grade.
